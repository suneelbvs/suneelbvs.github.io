## Summary

Director of AI & Drug Design leveraging machine learning — particularly deep
learning — to address complex problems in chemistry and drug discovery. Focused
on AI-driven molecular design, explainable AI for drug design, and deep learning
for structure-based discovery.

## Research interests

- AI-driven molecular design (generative models, *de novo* design, fragment expansion)
- Explainable / interpretable AI for medicinal chemistry
- Structure-based drug discovery (docking, free-energy methods + ML)
- Cheminformatics, QSAR/QSPR, molecular representation learning
- Data-driven toxicity forecasting
